Background

Climate crisis is one of the top problems our world is facing currently. To tackle this crisis, countries around the globe have been trying to lower their emission rates.

As a team, we are interested in using machine learning to help predict the future emissions from countries based on how they generate energy in the past years.

We want to discover whether or not the current efforts the countries put in are enough to meet their emission goal in 2030 based on the Paris 2015 Country Climate Pledges.

We are also interested in exploring countries’ yearly temperature change and to discover when the emission goals are and/or aren’t predicted to be met, what will be the prediction for our future temperature.
